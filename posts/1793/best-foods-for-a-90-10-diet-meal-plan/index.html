1. Introduction to 90 10 Diet Meal Plan#

The 90 10 Diet Meal Plan focuses on eating whole foods in their natural state, as close to their natural state as possible. By following a 90 10 Diet Meal Plan, you can gain numerous health benefits as well as maintain your desired bodyweight. Here are some of the best foods to include in your meal plan.

Whole grains are nutrient-rich and will give you a steady source of energy throughout the day. Try to make one-third to one-half of your grain servings whole grains like oatmeal, barley, brown rice, quinoa, or buckwheat. These are great for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or as a snack.

Fruit is a great source of natural sugars and essential vitamins and minerals. Aim to consume two to four servings of fruit every day. Eating apples, bananas, oranges, and berries, either raw or cooked, is an easy way to get in your daily fruit servings.

Vegetables are packed with essential vitamins and minerals that our body needs to stay healthy. Eating 3-5 servings of vegetables per day is a great way to get in all the essential nutrients. Cruciferous vegetables like broccoli, Brussels sprouts, and cauliflower are some of the most nutrient-dense veggies that you can add to your diet.

Protein sources are essential for maintaining muscle mass and keeping you full. Choose lean proteins such as beans, nuts, seeds, tofu, fish, and poultry. Having a few servings of protein with each meal or snack will keep your energy levels up and your appetite satisfied.
