1. Overview of ‘All Quiet on the Western Front’#

All Quiet on the Western Front, a novel by Erich Maria Remarque, paints a vivid picture of the brutality and tragedy of World War I through the eyes of a young German soldier. Despite being a novel, the horror and sadness the soldiers feel have been described in a way that it’s impossible to remain unaffected. Remarque has masterfully mixed the horror of war and the compassionate feelings of soldiers to create an all-encompassing view of the conflict. He has employed a wide variety of techniques – from the use of repetition to eloquent dialogue – to bring the characters and their predicament to life. All Quiet on the Western Front is an emotionally charged masterpiece of war literature that will continue to inspire and move readers for generations to come.
