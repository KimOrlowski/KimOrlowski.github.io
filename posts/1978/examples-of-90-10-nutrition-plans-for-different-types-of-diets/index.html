1. Explanation of What a 90/10 Nutrition Plan is#

A 90/10 nutrition plan is a dietary approach that focuses on consuming nutrient-dense foods 90 percent of the time and allowing yourself 10 percent of your daily caloric intake to indulge in less nutritious foods. It is important to note that portion control is still key when following this type of plan. Examples of 90/10 nutrition plans for different types of diets include vegan, vegetarian, ketogenic, paleo, and Mediterranean.

For a vegan diet, a 90/10 plan could include 90 percent whole grains, nuts, seeds, fruits, and vegetables such as quinoa, oats, walnuts, chia seeds, apples, and kale. These foods should be complemented with 10 percent plant-based proteins such as tofu, tempeh, and seitan.

A vegetarian 90/10 plan is similar to vegan but includes dairy and eggs. Examples of food combinations would include 90 percent legumes, grains, dairy, and eggs supplemented with 10 percent nuts, seeds, and fruits.

A 90/10 ketogenic plan involves consuming 90 percent fatty proteins and low-carb vegetables such as eggs, avocado, and salmon as well as 10 percent low-carb fruits such as berries and melon.

Following a paleo 90/10 plan requires focusing on 90 percent sources of animal proteins and vegetables such as grass-fed beef, free-range chicken, sweet potatoes, and greens, and the remaining 10 percent of snacks should come from nuts, seeds, and fruit.

Finally, a Mediterranean 90/10 nutrition plan involves 90 percent of plant-based proteins, vegetables, whole grains, nuts, and seeds, and the remaining 10 percent should come from olive oil, fish, eggs, and poultry.
