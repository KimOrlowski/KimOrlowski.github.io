1. Explanation of the 90/10 Nutrition Strategy#

The 90/10 Nutrition Strategy is a popular health and dietary lifestyle for achieving long-term health goals. It works on the simple principle of eating healthy, clean, nutritious foods 90% of the time, allowing for the other 10% to come from more indulgent, sugar-filled treats. This strategy allows consumers to still enjoy indulgences without having to go off their diet completely. The theory is that as long as the 90/10 ratio is followed, there is less guilt associated with eating unhealthy foods. This allows consumers to indulge in their favorite foods while still maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

The 90/10 strategy is attractive to people who are trying to make sustainable, lifelong changes in their diet. It sets realistic expectations and doesn’t deprive people of their favorite indulgences, allowing for a more balanced approach to dieting and fewer instances of ‘dieting fatigue.’ In addition to this, the 10% indulgence also helps to minimize cravings and binges which can lead to undoing all the hard work of the 90%.

The 90/10 strategy also helps to build healthy eating habits. By consistently following the 90/10 ratio, people are able to recognize what foods should be eaten more often and what should be avoided more often. This helps to instill healthy habits which can carry into the future.

Finally, the 90/10 strategy is a great way to practice mindful eating. By following the 90/10 ratio, people are encouraged to be mindful of their choices and to be present in the moment when indulging. This helps to break unhealthy patterns of mindless eating and establish a better relationship with food.
