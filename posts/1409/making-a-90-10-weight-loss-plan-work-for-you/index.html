The key to making a 90/10 weight loss plan work for you is to have a plan and stay consistent. First, start by deciding which days of the week you’d like to designate as your “treat days”. Then, write down a list of healthy meals and snacks that you enjoy, and plan out what you’d like to eat for the week. Second, when it comes to treating yourself, enjoy every bite! On your designated treat days, splurge on something that you really enjoy. It’s not just about the food, it’s also about the experience. When you splurge, savor every bite and really enjoy it.

Making a 90/10 weight loss plan work for you isn’t just about eating healthy 90% of the time and having an occasional treat 10% of the time. It’s also about finding balance, staying consistent, and truly enjoying your treats when you do indulge. By following these steps, you can make a weight loss plan work for you and reach your desired outcome.
