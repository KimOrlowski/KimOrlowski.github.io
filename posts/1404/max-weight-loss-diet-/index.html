1. Introduction to 90-10 Diet#

The 90-10 Diet is a popular diet program that involves eating a combination of 90% whole, unprocessed foods and 10% allowing for treats and indulgences. By following the 90/10 diet, you can still enjoy your favorite treats in moderation while also enjoying the health benefits of eating whole, unprocessed foods. The key is to keep the indulgences to a minimum so that overall healthy eating is the priority. Here’s what you should know about the 90-10 Diet and how to make it work for you.

For the 90 percent of the diet, you’ll be eating whole, unprocessed foods. This includes lots of fruits and vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, and low-sugar snacks. The key is to choose minimally processed foods whenever possible. When deciding what to eat, opt for nourishing options like avocado toast and muesli instead of processed options like cereal and snacks.

When it comes to the 10 percent of the diet, you can treat yourself to some indulgent food. But here’s the important part – the treats should be consumed in moderation. You should keep your splurge foods to around 200-300 calories per day, and you should choose healthier options whenever possible. Some examples of healthy treats include a glass of wine, a piece of dark chocolate, or some frozen yogurt.

To make the 90-10 Diet work for you, it’s important to plan ahead. Stock your kitchen with plenty of healthy whole foods and plan healthy meals for each day of the week. That way you’ll have easy access to healthy food when you’re feeling hungry. Additionally, make sure to have some healthy snacks on hand so that you can avoid reaching for unhealthy packaged snacks when you’re in a pinch.

By following the 90-10 Diet, you can still enjoy some of your favorite treats while also prioritizing your health. Just make sure to keep your indulgences to a minimum and focus on choosing healthy, whole foods whenever possible.
