1. Explain what a 90/10 diet is#

A 90/10 diet is an eating plan that encourages achieving dietary goals by eating healthfully 90% of the time and allowing for more leniency the remaining 10%. This plan focuses on eating nutrient-rich, whole foods such as fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains and healthy fats. Processed and refined foods are limited and only allowed 10% of the time. This plan is an excellent way to reach health and weight goals since it provides structure while still allowing for occasional treats. It helps people become more mindful of their food choices and encourages healthier eating most of the time. Furthermore, it is easy to follow as it does not require cutting out entire food groups or counting calories. For those looking to improve their health, the 90/10 diet could be a great starting point.

3. Steps to reach your goals with a 90/10 diet#

The 90/10 diet is an eating method that has become increasingly popular among fitness-minded individuals who are looking to reach their fitness goals. Following these three steps can help you use the 90/10 diet to reach your health and fitness goals. Paying attention to portion sizes, eating mostly whole foods, and allowing yourself to indulge in treats can all contribute to your journey to a healthier and happier you.
